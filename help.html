Shifted - Help

What is Shifted?

Shifted is a Mac application intended to increase your productivity by allowing you to self-assign a task to your Caps Lock key, while delegating the Caps Lock function to the Shift keys. Shifted runs in the background and, besides changing the preferences every so often, will require no additional input from you.
